Lasagna Recipe

Recipe Description

Indulge in the comforting layers of flavors with this timeless lasagna recipe. Starting with tender lasagna noodles, each layer is carefully assembled with a rich tomato sauce, savory ground beef or Italian sausage, and creamy ricotta cheese mixed with fragrant herbs. Generously sprinkled with a blend of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, each bite offers a perfect balance of textures and tastes. Whether served as a cozy family dinner or for entertaining guests, this lasagna is sure to delight palates and warm hearts with its hearty and satisfying essence.

Versatile and adaptable, this lasagna recipe invites creativity and personalization. Experiment with different fillings such as spinach and mushrooms for a vegetarian twist, or add layers of roasted vegetables for extra depth and freshness. Make ahead and freeze for busy weeknights, or bake fresh for a comforting meal that brings everyone together. With its timeless appeal and endless possibilities, this lasagna recipe is a true culinary classic that never fails to impress.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles (about 12-16 sheets)
  • 1 pound (450g) ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 ounces) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 15 ounces (425g) ricotta cheese
  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Fresh parsley or basil for garnish (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven: Preheat your oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles: Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions until they are al dente (firm to the bite). Drain and set aside.
  3. Prepare the sauce: In a large skillet or saucepan, brown the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat, breaking it up with a spoon as it cooks. Once browned, remove any excess fat from the pan. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 10-15 minutes, allowing the flavors to meld together.
  4. Prepare the cheese mixture: In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, egg, and grated Parmesan cheese. Mix until well combined.
  5. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a thin layer of the tomato sauce on the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange a layer of cooked lasagna noodles over the sauce. Spread half of the ricotta cheese mixture evenly over the noodles. Sprinkle with a layer of shredded mozzarella cheese. Repeat the layers, ending with a final layer of noodles topped with the remaining sauce and shredded mozzarella cheese.
  6. Bake the lasagna: Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Then, remove the foil and continue baking for an additional 15-20 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly and the edges are golden brown.
  7. Let it rest: Allow the lasagna to cool for 10-15 minutes before slicing. This allows the layers to set and makes it easier to serve.
  8. Serve and enjoy: Garnish the lasagna with fresh parsley or basil, if desired, and slice into portions. Serve warm and enjoy your delicious homemade lasagna!
